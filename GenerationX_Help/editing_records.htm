Editing people

You can edit a person's vital information by double clicking their name in the list of individuals or by selecting their name in the list of individuals and clicking the Edit toolbar item or selecting Edit Record from the Record menu.

If you change a person's parents, links to the precious parents are destroyed.

Adding/editing events

You can add information for many events in a person's life via the Add Individual Info menu under the Record menu. You can even create custom events for things GEDCOM doesn't cover. You can edit existing events by double clicking them in the details drawer or by selecting them and clicking the Edit toolbar item or selecting Edit Record from the Record menu.
