Navigating your data

You can navigate your GEDCOM data in any of four modes; Individual, Family, Pedogree, and Descendants. You can switch between modes using the tabs across the top of the main window.

Individual Mode
Individual mode is what you see wh[en GenX launches. Here you can browse vital information for the people in your database.

Family Mode
Family mode lets you browse the family records in your database. Family records keep track of links between spouses, parents, and children.

Pedigree Mode
Pedigree mode presents a brief view of three generations of ancestors for a given person.

Descendants Mode
Descendants view presents you with an expandable list of descendants for a give person. Spouses are indicated with a '+'. When you wxpand o a spouse, any existing children are listed.
